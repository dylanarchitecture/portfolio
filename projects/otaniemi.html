Otaniemi Chapel

Type Academic · Year 1
Location Espoo, Finland
Year 2021
Program Sacred Architecture · Light · Site
Description

A chapel proposal for the Otaniemi campus in Espoo, Finland — a first-year design exercise in spatial sequence, light, and material. The project takes as its starting point the landscape of the site: the pine forest, the granite outcrops, the quality of Nordic winter light.

The chapel is organised as a procession — a sequence of thresholds that move from the public landscape through a compressed anteroom and into the main gathering space. Light enters from a single high opening, moving across the wall through the day. The model was the primary design tool.

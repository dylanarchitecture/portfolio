02 — Professional Practice

Ailtireacht

Type Professional Practice
Location Dublin, Ireland
Year 2024
Program Residential · Architectural Production
Description

Architectural production work with Ailtireacht, a Dublin-based practice working across residential and civic projects. The work encompassed drawing production, model-making, site documentation, and design development across several concurrent commissions.

The experience foregrounded the relationship between design intent and built reality — the translation from drawn line to constructed joint, from section to material assembly. The drawings produced here are working documents: precise, communicative, and rooted in the logic of construction.

03 — Arts Research

CoLab81-7

Type Arts Research
Location Dublin, Ireland
Year 2024
Program Research · Exhibition · Mapping
Description

CoLab81-7 is a collaborative research and exhibition initiative investigating the spatial and institutional legacy of places of incarceration across Ireland. The project traces the physical history of former industrial schools, reformatories, and county homes — buildings whose forms encoded systems of control and whose landscapes still carry that mark.

The work produced includes archival mapping, site documentation, and exhibition design — translating research into spatial form. The Rún map traces movements and routes across institutional Ireland; the village and institution maps locate these sites within their wider territorial contexts.
